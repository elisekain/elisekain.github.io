--- date: 2017-5-01 year: 2017 title: Kevin Chang selected for Neutron Summer School at NIST text: Graduate student Kevin Chang has been invited to participate in the twenty-third annual Center for High Resolution Neutron Scattering "Summer School on Methods and Applications of Neutron Spectroscopy", which is held at the NIST Center for Neutron Research in Gaithersburg, MD.
