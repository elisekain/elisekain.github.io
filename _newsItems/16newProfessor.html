--- date: 2016-3-01 year: 2016 title: Prof. Geise selected to receive the Engineering Conferences International New Professor Award text: Prof. Geise was selected to receive the Engineering Conferences International (ECI) New Professor Award. This award is made to assistant professors to develop future talent by enabling their attendance at an ECI meeting. Prof. Geise will present the group's research at the Advanced Membrane Technology VII meeting in September 2016. ---
