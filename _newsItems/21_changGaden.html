--- date: 2021-04-19 year: 2021 title: Kevin Chang wins the Elmer L. Gaden Excellence in Doctoral Study Award text: Congratulations to Dr. Kevin Chang on winning the 2021 Elmer L. Gaden Excellence in Doctoral Study Award! This award was established by the publishing firm of John Wiley & Sons, Inc. to recognize the late UVA Chemical Engineering Professor, Elmer L. Gaden, Jr., on the occasion of his retirement as Editor of the journal Biotechnology & Bioengineering. The award criterion stipulates excellence in research, especially in independent planning and accomplishment. Additionally, graduate teaching assistanceship service to the department is considered. Well done Dr. Chang! image: chang.jpg ---
