--- date: 2021-5-31 year: 2021 title: Saringi Agata Publishes Research on a Primer to Avoid Membrane Defects text: Saringi Agata's research to investigate the use of poly(vinyl alcohol) as a primer layer to enable layer-by-layer sequential deposition of polyamide membranes on microporous supports was recently accepted for publication in the Journal of Applied Polymer Science!
