--- date: 2017-4-01 year: 2017 title: Andrew Biedermann wins SEAS Outstanding Student Award text: Fourth year undergraduate researcher Andrew Biedermann won the Outstanding Student Award. This award, which is presented by the UVA School of Engineering and Applied Science, recognizes fourth year undergraduate students who have excelled in the areas of academics, leadership, and service. Drew has been an active undergraduate researcher in the group for three years now, and he will be missed next year as he begins graduate school in chemcial engineering at MIT.
