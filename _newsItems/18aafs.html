--- date: 2018-11-4 year: 2018 title: Prof. Geise Attended the 6th Arab-American Frontiers Symposium text: Prof. Geise was invited to be a water systems delegate at the 6th Arab-American Frontiers of Science, Engineering, and Medicine Symposium. Organized by the U.S. National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine and the Kuwait Foundation for the Advancement of Science (KFAS), the meeting was held in Kuwait City, Kuwait. Prof. Geise presented a poster to highlight the group's desalination and water purification membrane research. image: aafs.jpg ---
