--- date: 2016-9-01 year: 2016 title: Drew Biedermann wins the AIChE Donald F. and Mildred Topp Othmer Scholarship text: Fourth year undergraduate student Drew Biedermann was awarded a 2016-2017 Donald F. and Mildred Topp Othmer Scholarship by the American Institute of Chemical Engineers. This award is presented annually to 15 AIChE undergraduate student members on the basis of academic achievement and involvement in student chapter activities. The award will be presented at the 2016 AIChE Annual Student Conference in San Francisco on Sunday, November 13. ---
