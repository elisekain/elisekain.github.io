--- date: 2017-6-01 year: 2017 title: Geise Wins UVA SEAS Research Innovation Award text: UVA ChE Professors Geise, Koenig, and Paolucci won a Research Innovation Award from the School of Engineering and Applied Science at UVA. The joint award will support multidisciplinary research on lithium ion transport in polymers for non-aqueous flow battery systems. ---
